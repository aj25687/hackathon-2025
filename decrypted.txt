Welcome to the Westchester Cadet Squadron 1 Server! This is meant to only be for the members in the squadron. DO NOT give out an invite link to people that are not officially affiliated in the squadron. 
Follow all CAP core values. Act in this server the same way you would act in the squadron during meetings. 
No Harassment/Bullying
Harassment of any form is unacceptable. This includes insults, threats, discrimination, or degrading remarks based on race, religion, culture, gender, or identity. Lighthearted banter is fine, but know the line. Everyone deserves a safe and welcoming environment here.
No Offensive/NSFW Content
This community is built around respect and enjoyment. Content that includes nudity, explicit material, hate speech, offensive language, like profanity is  strictly prohibited or anything inappropriate is strictly forbidden. Rage-bait, shock value posts, or content meant to provoke negative reactions will not be tolerated. Keep discussions and posts respectful, safe, and suitable for all members.
Respect Discord’s Rules
All members must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service. This means you must be 13 years or older, avoid third-party modifications to the Discord client, and play fairly. Violations of Discord’s official rules will not only get you banned from this server but may result in action from Discord itself.
