Shelley has acknowledged that Wordsworth and Coleridge’s works have been some of her inspirations for Frankenstein, so their complex relationship could have also influenced Shelley’s characters’ relationships in the book. Yaoi can be found everywhere, and Shelley makes good use of the male-on-male angst she encounters to incorporate all kinds of BL into Frankenstein, but options at her time were pretty limited, however, so Shelley had to resort to more subtle ways to incorporate yaoi into her writing, which can be uncovered by reading the book through the lens of a modern day BL manga.
